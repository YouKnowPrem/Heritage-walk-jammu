Experience centuries of history, architecture, and culture...

Experience centuries of history, architecture, and culture as we explore the magnificent Mubarak Mandi - once the royal residence of the Dogra dynasty - and wander through the bustling lanes of Old City with its traditional markets, ancient temples, and timeless stories.

Our heritage walks combine historical insights with cultural immersion, followed by an engaging symposium featuring local historians, architects, and cultural experts.
